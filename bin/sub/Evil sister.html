Evil sister＼（〇_ｏ）／

In the heart of the dense woods stood a forgotten village, its name was erased from the maps and memory of the outside world. Stories whispered of its abandonment, tales of an unspeakable curse that had befallen its inhabitants.
Among those who still dared to speak of it, the legend of two sisters, twins born of darkness, lingered like a shadow.

Lena and Elara were their names, but to the villagers, they were known simply as the Sisters of Sorrow.
Their arrival was shrouded in mystery, their origins obscured by whispers and superstition. With raven-black hair and eyes as dark as the night, they cast an eerie presence over the village from the moment they set foot upon its cursed soil.

first, the villagers welcomed them, believing them to be benevolent strangers seeking refuge from the world beyond the woods.
But as time passed, whispers turned to screams, and the true nature of the sisters began to reveal itself.

It started subtly, with strange occurrences plaguing the village: crops withered, livestock perished, and children vanished without a trace.
Fear gripped the hearts of the villagers as they realized the sinister truth—they were at the mercy of forces beyond their understanding.

As the days stretched into weeks, the sisters’ hold over the village tightened like a noose around its neck. They wove spells of darkness and despair, twisting the minds of the innocent to serve their malevolent purposes.
Those who dared to defy them were met with swift and merciless punishment, their souls consumed by the sisters’ insatiable hunger for power.

Soon, the village stood on the brink of extinction, its streets silent and its buildings crumbling into decay.
The sisters, now drunk on the suffering they had wrought, reveled in their dominance over the hollow shell of what was once a thriving community.

But amidst the despair, a glimmer of hope remained—a lone survivor, a young girl named Eva, who had escaped the sisters’ grasp and fled into the depths of the woods.
With courage as her guide and determination as her shield, she embarked on a perilous journey to uncover the secrets of the sisters’ dark past and put an end to their reign of terror once and for all.

Through treacherous terrain and whispered warnings, Eva pressed on, her resolve unbroken by the horrors that lurked in the shadows.
And as she finally reached the heart of the forest, she confronted the sisters in a showdown that would decide the fate of the village and all who called it home.

In a battle that raged between light and darkness, good and evil, Eva emerged victorious, her bravery shining like a beacon in the blackest of nights.
With the sisters vanquished and their malevolent influence lifted, the curse that had plagued the village was broken, and life slowly began to return to its barren streets.

But though the nightmare was over, the memory of the sisters would linger like a scar upon the land—a reminder of the darkness that dwells within us all, waiting to be unleashed upon the world.
And as the villagers rebuilt their shattered lives, they vowed never to forget the price of their salvation, nor the courage of the one who had dared to defy the shadows and bring light to the darkness that had consumed them all.
